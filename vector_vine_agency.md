## 💡 ABA Pro Coach — B2B SaaS Opportunity

**What it is:**
A mobile web app built for Behavior Technicians (BTs) to study for the RBT exam and support real ABA sessions. Features include flashcards, quizzes, a glossary, session helper tools, progress tracking, and achievements.

**The Problem it Solves:**
ABA clinics spend significant time and money onboarding and training new BTs. Most training is done through expensive third-party platforms or informal methods. ABA Pro Coach keeps training in-house, standardized, and accessible from any phone.

**Target Customer:**
ABA clinics, therapy centers, and school districts that employ Behavior Technicians. There are thousands of ABA providers across the US facing the same onboarding challenge.

---

## 💰 Pricing Strategy

### Option 1 — Sell to Your Employer First
- **Monthly SaaS fee:** $5–$15/user/month
  - 20 BTs × $10/month = $200/month = $2,400/year (recurring)
- **One-time licensing fee:** $2,000–$8,000 depending on org size
- **Setup + maintenance retainer:** $200–$500/month

**Recommended:** Monthly per-user fee. Recurring revenue is more valuable than a one-time sale.

### Option 2 — Scale to Other Clinics
- Use your employer as Case Study #1
- Approach other ABA clinics with proof: "Here's what I built, here's how it performed, here's the price."
- At 10 clinics × $200/month = $2,000/month = $24,000/year

---

## 🚫 Why NOT App Store + Ads (for now)
- RBT/BT audience is too niche for meaningful ad revenue
- App Store review process adds friction and cost ($99/year Apple dev fee)
- B2B direct sales to clinics is faster and more lucrative at this stage
- Revisit App Store if scaling to a national audience
